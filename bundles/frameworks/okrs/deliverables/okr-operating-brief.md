# OKR Operating Brief

## Required Sections

1. **Source note**: Name the public framework source category, user-provided local evidence, and missing evidence.
2. **Context**: State the team, owner, time horizon, and strategic priority.
3. **OKRs**: Present objectives and key results, or a review of provided OKRs.
4. **Evidence table**: For each key result, show metric source, baseline, target, owner, and verification status.
5. **Initiatives and dependencies**: Separate work bets from outcome evidence.
6. **Policy fit**: State cadence, scoring, committed/aspirational treatment, and local policy assumptions.
7. **Risks and gaps**: Identify weak metrics, perverse incentives, dependency risks, and missing data.
8. **Confirmation needed**: List live-system, HR, employee-communication, or executive-commitment actions that need explicit confirmation.

## Quality Bar

The brief is acceptable only when it avoids invented local facts and makes the next review decision clear.
