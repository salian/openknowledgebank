# Sprint Operating Review

1. Identify the sprint under review and the user's decision.
2. Inspect the Sprint Goal, selected backlog, Definition of Done, increment evidence, and event outputs when provided.
3. Compare the available evidence to the Scrum Guide lens.
4. Separate delivery facts from interpretations: completed work, uncompleted work, discovered risk, stakeholder feedback, quality evidence, and process learning.
5. Avoid treating velocity, story points, or ticket count as sufficient evidence of value or done work.
6. Produce a brief with findings, missing evidence, risks, and next actions.
