# Scrum Source Checks

Before finalizing Scrum guidance, verify:

- Official Scrum terms come from the Scrum Guide.
- Team-specific claims come from user-provided evidence.
- Tool-specific claims are based on inspected tool configuration or are labeled as assumptions.
- Metrics, velocity, estimates, and board states are not treated as universal Scrum requirements.
- Changes to live systems, staffing responsibilities, commitments, releases, or stakeholder communications require confirmation.
