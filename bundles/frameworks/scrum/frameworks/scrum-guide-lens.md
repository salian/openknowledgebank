# Scrum Guide Lens

Use this lens when a user asks whether a team is using Scrum well, whether a process change is compatible with Scrum, or how to improve sprint operations.

## Source-Grounded Checks

- Accountabilities: Product Owner, Scrum Master, and Developers.
- Events: Sprint, Sprint Planning, Daily Scrum, Sprint Review, and Sprint Retrospective.
- Artifacts: Product Backlog, Sprint Backlog, and Increment.
- Commitments: Product Goal, Sprint Goal, and Definition of Done.
- Values: Commitment, Focus, Openness, Respect, and Courage.

## Local Evidence Needed

- Current team accountabilities and decision rights.
- Product goal and sprint goal evidence.
- Product Backlog and Sprint Backlog examples or descriptions.
- Definition of Done and increment evidence.
- Sprint review and retrospective outputs.
- Delivery constraints, compliance constraints, release process, and stakeholder expectations.

## Classification

For each finding, classify it as:

- source-aligned and locally evidenced;
- source-aligned but locally unverified;
- local adaptation with stated rationale;
- potential gap needing more evidence;
- risky change requiring confirmation.
