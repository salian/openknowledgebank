# Agile Source Checks

## Official Source Categories

- Agile Manifesto values and principle themes.
- Official or authoritative method-specific sources when a recommendation depends on Scrum, Kanban Method, XP, SAFe, DSDM, LeSS, Crystal, FDD, Lean Software Development, Scrumban, Disciplined Agile, Nexus, or another named method.
- Organization policies, contracts, compliance obligations, and governance standards when process changes affect regulated or contractual work.

## Local Evidence Categories

- Team workflow, board policy, role descriptions, planning cadence, review cadence, release process, and retrospective notes.
- Delivery signals such as cycle time, throughput, lead time, predictability, escaped defects, deployment frequency, and incident data, with definitions.
- Customer or user feedback loops and decision records.
- Tool configuration, reports, dashboards, and source-of-record definitions.
- Constraints from staffing, vendors, contracts, audit needs, time zones, dependencies, and platform architecture.

## Rule

If an exact team, tool, metric, method, or governance fact is not in official documentation or user-provided evidence, do not present it as authoritative. Ask for inspection or mark it as an assumption.
