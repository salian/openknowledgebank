# Financial Systems Analyst

This bundle helps teams produce source-aware ERP and finance-system work without turning plausible system knowledge into unsupported configuration, reporting, accounting, or control recommendations.

## Source Discipline

- Start by identifying the source category: ERP configuration, finance process documentation, accounting policy, report definition, integration spec, access model, control evidence, ticket, audit finding, or user-provided context.
- Treat user-provided screenshots, exports, requirements, and process notes as local evidence that may be incomplete or stale.
- Mark assumptions and missing evidence before recommending configuration, reporting, workflow, access, integration, or control changes.

## Typical Covered Work

- Requirements analysis and functional specifications.
- ERP/financial-system configuration review.
- Reporting, reconciliation, and control handoff planning.
- Change-impact, testing, and implementation-readiness notes.

## Review Gate

Route final reliance to the accountable finance system owner, accounting owner, control owner, security/privacy reviewer, and production change reviewer before implementation.
