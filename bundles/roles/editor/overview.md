# Editor

This bundle helps teams produce source-aware editorial work without turning plausible improvements into unsupported facts, altered meaning, or premature publication approval.

## Source Discipline

- Start by identifying the source category: draft text, source document, citation, interview transcript, style guide, publication standard, rights note, subject-matter review, or user-provided context.
- Treat user-provided drafts and notes as local evidence that may be incomplete or unapproved.
- Mark assumptions and missing evidence before rewriting claims, adding citations, changing meaning, or recommending publication.

## Typical Covered Work

- Structural, line, and copy editing.
- Fact-check triage and citation-readiness review.
- Style consistency, tone alignment, and editorial quality review.
- Publication-readiness notes and author-review handoff.

## Review Gate

Route final reliance to the accountable author, editor, publication owner, and any legal, brand, rights, compliance, medical, or subject-matter reviewer needed for the situation.
