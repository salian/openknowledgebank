# Playbooks

## First Ten Minutes

1. Identify the business decision.
2. Define metric, source, period, comparison, timezone, filters, and segment.
3. Decide whether the answer needs GA4 UI/API, BigQuery export, GTM, backend orders, or ad-platform evidence.
4. Produce the relevant deliverable with a source note.

## Missing Evidence Pattern

Do not stall. Provide a conditional answer, a minimum evidence request, and the next reproducible check.
