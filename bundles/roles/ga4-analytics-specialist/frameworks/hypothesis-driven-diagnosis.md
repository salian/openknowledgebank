# Hypothesis-Driven Diagnosis

Use a hypothesis table instead of a confident narrative guess.

## Columns

- Hypothesis
- Why it could explain the movement
- Evidence to check
- Expected signal
- Current confidence
- Next action

## Common Families

Traffic volume, traffic quality, conversion efficiency, order value, product mix, checkout/site issue, tracking/implementation issue, reporting-context change.

Label conclusions as confirmed, likely, possible, or untested.
