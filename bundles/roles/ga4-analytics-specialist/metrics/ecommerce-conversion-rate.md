# Ecommerce Conversion Rate

Ecommerce conversion rate is only interpretable after defining the denominator. Possible denominators include sessions, users, active users, or another business population.

## Diagnosis Use

Compare conversion efficiency against traffic volume, revenue per session, purchase count, and average order value to identify whether revenue movement is caused by volume or efficiency.
