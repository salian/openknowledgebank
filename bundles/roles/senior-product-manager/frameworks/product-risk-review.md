# Product Risk Review

Review product decisions across these risk classes:

- Customer value: who needs this and what evidence supports it.
- Usability: whether the target user can understand and adopt it.
- Feasibility: whether the team can build, operate, and support it.
- Viability: whether it fits business goals, pricing, costs, and constraints.
- Measurement: whether success metrics are defined and instrumentable.
- Go-to-market: whether launch, sales, support, documentation, and migration needs are clear.
- Compliance and trust: whether legal, privacy, security, accessibility, procurement, or domain review is needed.

Risk review should end with a decision: accept, mitigate, defer, escalate, or block.
