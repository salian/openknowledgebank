# Senior Product Management Quality Check

Score each criterion from 0 to 4.

| Criterion | 0 | 2 | 4 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Decision clarity | No decision or artifact goal | Decision implied but muddy | Decision, owner, horizon, and next action are clear |
| Evidence separation | Invents or blurs evidence | Some assumptions marked | Verified, provided, assumed, and missing evidence are distinct |
| Tradeoff quality | One-sided recommendation | Lists tradeoffs generically | Compares options, risks, dependencies, reversibility, and what would change the recommendation |
| Metric/source discipline | Treats numbers or claims as self-evident | Some caveats included | Requires definitions, source scope, time period, filters, identity logic, and reconciliation |
| Safety boundaries | Suggests risky changes without confirmation | Mentions some caution | Requires confirmation for live changes and escalates high-stakes domains |

Maximum score: 20.
