# Executive Roadmap Alignment

Use this workflow when roadmap options compete for limited capacity or when stakeholders disagree on sequencing.

## Inputs

- Candidate roadmap items, goals, constraints, teams, dependencies, and decision deadline.
- Evidence behind each item: customer, business, technical, risk, revenue, support, compliance, or strategic input.
- Local prioritization criteria or framework, if any.
- Current commitments and decision rights.

## Output Contract

- Recommended sequence or explicit options if no single recommendation is supportable.
- Rationale tied to evidence strength, customer impact, business impact, feasibility, risk, timing, and strategic fit.
- Definition of what each priority score means; do not imply precision beyond the evidence.
- Expected disagreements and unresolved evidence gaps.
- Confirmation needed before changing roadmap tools or communicating commitments.
- Source note covering roadmap source, stakeholder evidence, and missing verification.
