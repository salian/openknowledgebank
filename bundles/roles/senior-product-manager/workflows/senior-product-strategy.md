# Senior Product Strategy

Use this workflow when the user needs to clarify a product direction, opportunity, strategic bet, or ambiguous product problem.

## Inputs

- Decision to be made and decision owner.
- Target users, segments, business goals, constraints, and time horizon.
- Customer evidence, market evidence, support/sales signals, analytics, and known risks.
- Current strategy, roadmap, team capacity, dependencies, and non-negotiables.

## Output Contract

- Direct strategy recommendation or decision framing.
- Evidence table: verified source facts, user-provided evidence, assumptions, and missing verification.
- Strategic options with tradeoffs, risks, dependencies, and reversibility.
- Recommended next decision, experiment, discovery step, or escalation.
- Source note naming local sources used and missing sources required before commitment.

## Quality Bar

The output should make the strategy easier to decide, not merely longer. It should expose the strongest opposing argument and the evidence that would change the recommendation.
