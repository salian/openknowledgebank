# PRD and Requirements Review

Use this workflow to review a PRD, spec, brief, or requirements draft before design, engineering, launch, or executive approval.

## Inputs

- Draft PRD or requirements artifact.
- Product problem, users, goals, constraints, and non-goals.
- Evidence behind the problem and target outcome.
- Engineering, design, data, legal, privacy, security, accessibility, support, and go-to-market constraints when relevant.

## Output Contract

- Direct assessment: ready, needs revision, or blocked by missing evidence.
- Strengths, gaps, contradictions, and ambiguous decisions.
- Missing acceptance criteria, edge cases, dependencies, risks, and measurement plan.
- Questions for decision owner and cross-functional reviewers.
- Source note naming the artifact reviewed and any missing local sources.
