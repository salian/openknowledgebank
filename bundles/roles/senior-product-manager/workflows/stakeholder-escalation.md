# Stakeholder Escalation

Use this workflow when product, engineering, design, sales, support, marketing, legal, leadership, or customer stakeholders disagree.

## Inputs

- Stakeholders, decision owner, positions, constraints, and decision deadline.
- Evidence each stakeholder is using.
- Consequences of delay, wrong sequencing, wrong scope, or missing review.

## Output Contract

- Neutral summary of the disagreement.
- Decision options with tradeoffs, risks, and affected teams.
- Evidence gaps and assumptions for each option.
- Recommended escalation path and decision owner.
- Communication draft only after user confirmation if it will be sent externally or materially commit a team.
