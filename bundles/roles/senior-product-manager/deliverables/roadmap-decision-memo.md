# Roadmap Decision Memo

## Required Sections

- Decision needed and deadline.
- Recommended sequencing or options.
- Rationale by customer value, business impact, feasibility, risk, timing, and strategic fit.
- Evidence strength and missing verification.
- Dependencies and capacity assumptions.
- Stakeholder concerns and escalation path.
- Source note.

Do not present tentative dates, owners, or scope as committed unless the user provided a source of record.
