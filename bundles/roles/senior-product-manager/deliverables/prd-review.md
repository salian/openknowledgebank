# PRD Review

## Required Sections

- Readiness call: ready, needs revision, or blocked.
- What is clear.
- What is ambiguous or unsupported.
- Missing acceptance criteria, edge cases, dependencies, and non-goals.
- Measurement and rollout gaps.
- Review questions by function.
- Source note.

The review should improve decision quality without rewriting the PRD into unsupported certainty.
