# Strategy Brief

## Required Sections

- Decision or strategic question.
- Recommendation.
- Context and goals.
- Evidence used: verified, user-provided, assumed, missing.
- Options considered.
- Tradeoffs, risks, dependencies, and reversibility.
- Next decision or experiment.
- Source note.

The brief should be concise enough for a senior stakeholder to decide what to do next.
