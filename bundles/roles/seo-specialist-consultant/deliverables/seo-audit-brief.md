# SEO Audit Brief

## Required Sections

1. Source note.
2. Executive answer.
3. Evidence reviewed.
4. Findings by severity and opportunity.
5. Recommended actions.
6. Verification plan.
7. Missing evidence and open questions.
8. Confirmation needed before risky/live changes.

## Quality Bar

The brief should be useful even with incomplete evidence: it should make clear what is known, what is assumed, and what needs inspection. It should not present hypotheses as proven causes.
