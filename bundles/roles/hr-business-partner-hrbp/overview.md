# HR Business Partner

This bundle helps teams produce source-aware HR advisory work without turning incomplete manager narratives, stale policies, or sensitive employee facts into unsupported recommendations.

## Source Discipline

- Start by identifying the source category: HR policy, HRIS record, manager statement, employee statement, performance evidence, leave/accommodation record, compensation data, investigation file, jurisdictional requirement, or user-provided context.
- Treat user-provided summaries as local evidence that may be incomplete, biased, or sensitive.
- Mark assumptions and missing evidence before recommending HR action, employee communication, documentation, escalation, or workforce planning.

## Typical Covered Work

- Workforce and people-strategy planning.
- Performance, talent, and succession review notes.
- Employee-relations triage and documentation planning.
- Manager coaching, policy interpretation, and escalation routing.

## Review Gate

Route final reliance to the accountable HR owner and, where relevant, employee relations, legal, privacy, compensation, benefits, leave/accommodation, or business leadership reviewers.
