# Source-Aware HR Advisory Triage

Use this workflow before producing HR guidance, people-strategy notes, employee-relations documentation, or manager coaching.

## Steps

1. Confirm the decision: workforce plan, manager coaching, talent review, employee-relations triage, policy interpretation, or escalation note.
2. Identify the evidence set: policy, jurisdiction, HRIS record, role expectations, performance evidence, manager statement, employee statement, prior communications, and review owners.
3. Classify every important claim as verified from source, provided by manager/employee, assumed for draft, or missing.
4. Identify sensitive categories, privacy limits, legal/ER review needs, documentation standards, and communication risk before recommending action.
5. Produce a recommendation with open questions, owner review, and explicit confirmation before employee-facing or record-changing action.

## Output Contract

The final answer should include decision scope, evidence used, evidence missing, risk flags, recommended next action, owner review, confirmation requirements, and a Source note.
