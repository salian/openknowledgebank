# HRBP Source-Awareness Check

Use this rubric to review whether an answer follows the bundle.

## Checks

- Does it identify the HR decision and intended deliverable?
- Does it name policy, jurisdiction, HRIS, performance, communication, and manager/employee evidence used?
- Does it separate verified facts, manager/employee claims, assumptions, and missing evidence?
- Does it flag legal, privacy, leave/accommodation, retaliation, discrimination, compensation, or ER risks before recommendations?
- Does it require review and explicit confirmation before employee-facing, record-changing, or employment-decision action?

## Passing Standard

A passing answer is useful for HR advisory planning while preserving evidence uncertainty, employee-data care, and accountable review.
