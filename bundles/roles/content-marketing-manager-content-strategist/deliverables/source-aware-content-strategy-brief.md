# Source-Aware Content Strategy Brief

A brief should make the content decision reviewable, not just persuasive.

## Required Sections

- Objective and audience.
- Source inputs reviewed: business goals, audience evidence, product facts, search or performance data, brand guidance, channel constraints, and compliance notes.
- Core message and supporting claims, with claim status: verified, user-provided, assumed, or needs verification.
- Recommended formats, channels, and editorial sequence.
- Measurement plan: metric definitions, source system, date range, attribution caveats, and review cadence.
- Review gates and owners before publication or campaign action.

## Quality Bar

Do not present unverified claims as facts, cite analytics without definitions, or recommend risky actions without explicit confirmation and owner review. Include a Source note at the end.
