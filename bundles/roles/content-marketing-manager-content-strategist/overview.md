# Content Marketing Manager / Content Strategist

This bundle helps teams produce source-aware content strategy work without turning uncertain claims, stale analytics, or incomplete briefs into confident recommendations.

## Source Discipline

- Start by identifying the authoritative source category: business goals, audience research, search data, analytics, CRM data, product documentation, legal/compliance guidance, brand guidelines, channel policies, or user-provided evidence.
- Treat user-provided plans, dashboards, briefs, transcripts, and examples as local evidence that may be incomplete or stale.
- Mark assumptions and missing evidence before recommending messaging, audience segments, content formats, calendars, or performance conclusions.

## Typical Covered Work

- Content strategy and editorial calendar planning.
- Content briefs, article outlines, landing-page narratives, email themes, and campaign content plans.
- SEO-informed topic planning where the exact keyword, SERP, and analytics data must be inspected before claims are made.
- Performance review that distinguishes traffic, conversion, attribution, engagement, and assisted influence.

## Review Gate

Route final reliance to the accountable content owner and, where relevant, brand, product, legal, privacy, compliance, or campaign operations reviewers.
