# Technical Sales Representative Source-Awareness Check

Check that responses:

- name the relevant source category;
- separate role facts, local evidence, assumptions, and missing evidence;
- avoid invented product, customer, CRM, pricing, contract, or technical facts;
- reconcile conflicting inputs before deciding;
- require confirmation for customer-facing, commercial, system, export, or public-claim actions;
- route final reliance to qualified review.
