# Technical Sales Representative (Technical and Scientific Products)

This bundle helps teams handle technical-sales questions without overstating product, customer, commercial, or source facts.

## Source Discipline

- Start with the authoritative source category named in the source note.
- Treat user-provided customer notes, CRM exports, product docs, quote sheets, contracts, implementation plans, approved-claim libraries, and local context as local evidence, not as universal truth.
- Mark missing source text, missing local facts, and assumptions before giving a conclusion.

## Primary Authority

O*NET 41-4011.00 is the inspected role source for the occupation scope. Current product, customer, pricing, contract, delivery, scientific, engineering, safety, regulatory, and CRM facts must come from local source evidence.

## Typical Covered Matter

- Customer need discovery and qualification.
- Technical product-fit reasoning.
- Proposal and presentation preparation.
- Quotation, price, credit-term, and bid-specification review.
- Customer questions about products, availability, delivery, service contracts, and warranties.
- Customer record and sales-report review.
- Conflict reconciliation across CRM, spreadsheets, account teams, technical teams, contracts, or customer documents.

## Review Gate

Route final reliance to a qualified sales owner, product specialist, solution engineer, legal/commercial reviewer, or accountable business reviewer.
