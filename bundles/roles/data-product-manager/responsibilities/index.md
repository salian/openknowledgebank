# Responsibilities

- Identify data consumers, jobs-to-be-done, decision points, and measurable outcomes.
- Translate business and consumer needs into data-product requirements.
- Define product scope, non-goals, lifecycle state, ownership, and support expectations.
- Coordinate with data engineering, analytics engineering, data science, governance, security, privacy, and consuming teams.
- Review data contracts, metric definitions, quality expectations, freshness, lineage, access, and change-management needs.
- Prioritize roadmap options using value, risk, effort, dependency, quality, adoption, and trust evidence.
- Maintain source discipline: do not assert local schemas, metrics, or policies without evidence.
