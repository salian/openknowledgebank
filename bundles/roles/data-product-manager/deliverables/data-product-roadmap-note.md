# Data Product Roadmap Note

## Required Sections

- Roadmap recommendation.
- Source note.
- Decision owner, time horizon, constraints, and prioritization method.
- Options with consumer value, business value, quality/trust impact, risk, effort, dependencies, and reversibility.
- Verified evidence, assumptions, and missing verification.
- Items to do now, next, later, defer, deprecate, or split.
- Confirmation needed before updating live roadmap systems.

## Quality Bar

The note should support a decision without pretending unverified dates, effort, ROI, or adoption data are known.
