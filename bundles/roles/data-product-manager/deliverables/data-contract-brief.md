# Data Contract Brief

## Required Sections

- Readiness status.
- Source note.
- Producers, consumers, owners, source of record, and lifecycle state.
- Schema or entity scope, business meaning, accepted values, and metric implications.
- Quality, freshness, SLA/SLO, lineage, observability, and incident expectations.
- Access, privacy, retention, and governance constraints.
- Breaking-change policy, versioning, support, and escalation path.
- Verified facts, assumptions, missing verification, and required owner confirmations.

## Quality Bar

The brief should make contract gaps visible before consumers rely on the data. It should not approve a contract with unknown schema, ownership, quality, or change-management evidence.
