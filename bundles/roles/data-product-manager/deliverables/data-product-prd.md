# Data Product PRD

## Required Sections

- Direct readiness recommendation.
- Source note.
- Problem, consumers, outcome, scope, non-goals, and lifecycle state.
- Data sources, definitions, contracts, schemas, metric logic, and semantic-layer dependencies.
- Evidence table separating verified facts, user-provided evidence, assumptions, and missing verification.
- Quality, freshness, lineage, observability, incident, support, and trust requirements.
- Access, privacy, retention, governance, and compliance considerations.
- Requirements and acceptance criteria.
- Dependencies, rollout, migration, deprecation, change management, and support path.
- Risks, unresolved questions, and decision owners.

## Quality Bar

Every implementation-specific claim must trace to provided or inspected evidence. If evidence is absent, the PRD should ask for it rather than filling in plausible details.
