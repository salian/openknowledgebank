# Data Product Brief

## Required Sections

- Recommendation.
- Source note.
- Consumer, decision, job-to-be-done, and outcome.
- Current data asset or proposed data product.
- Evidence table: verified facts, user-provided evidence, assumptions, missing verification.
- Scope, non-goals, lifecycle state, owner, and support expectation.
- Quality, freshness, lineage, access, privacy, governance, and adoption risks.
- Options and next decisions.

## Quality Bar

The brief must not treat an unverified dataset, dashboard, model, or metric as product-ready. It must name the source evidence needed before a team commits to delivery.
