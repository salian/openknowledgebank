# Data Product Evidence Model

Use this framework to structure data-product decisions before drafting requirements, contracts, or roadmap recommendations.

## Evidence Areas

- Consumer: user, workflow, decision, frequency, pain, and success signal.
- Outcome: business value, product value, operational impact, and risk reduction.
- Source: source systems, schemas, lineage, refresh, transformations, ownership, and source of record.
- Definition: metric or entity meaning, grain, identity logic, filters, exclusions, and calculation method.
- Trust: quality checks, freshness, incidents, completeness, observability, and known limitations.
- Governance: classification, access, privacy, retention, compliance, stewardship, and approval path.
- Delivery: dependencies, engineering scope, rollout, support, change management, and lifecycle state.

## Output Rule

For each area, state what is verified, what the user provided, what is assumed for the draft, and what still needs verification.
