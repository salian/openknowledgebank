# Plan Data Product Roadmap

Use this workflow when a team needs to prioritize data-product improvements, launches, migrations, deprecations, or governance work.

## Inputs

- Candidate roadmap items and consumer impact.
- Outcome, adoption, quality, incident, support, and trust evidence.
- Dependency, effort, owner, risk, governance, and delivery constraints.
- Existing roadmap commitments and decision rights.

## Steps

1. Confirm the decision owner, time horizon, and constraints.
2. Classify evidence and missing verification.
3. Score or compare options using value, consumer impact, risk reduction, quality/trust improvement, urgency, effort, dependencies, and reversibility.
4. Identify prerequisites: source inspection, owner decisions, governance review, engineering discovery, or consumer validation.
5. Recommend a roadmap order with caveats and confirmation needs.

## Output Contract

- Direct roadmap recommendation.
- Source note.
- Prioritized options with evidence and assumptions.
- Dependencies, risks, missing verification, and owner confirmations.
- Items to defer, deprecate, or split.

Do not invent effort estimates, delivery dates, ROI, adoption, or incident history.
