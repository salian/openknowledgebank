# Prioritize Roadmap

Use this workflow when deciding what should move forward, what should wait, and what evidence is needed before sequencing product work.

## Inputs

- Candidate initiatives or roadmap items.
- Product strategy and current goals.
- User/customer evidence.
- Business impact evidence.
- Effort, capacity, dependencies, risk, and deadline constraints.
- Existing commitments and roadmap source of record.

## Steps

1. Confirm the prioritization decision and time horizon.
2. Normalize each option to the same level of detail.
3. Identify evidence quality for impact, reach, urgency, confidence, effort, risk, dependencies, and strategic fit.
4. Apply a prioritization lens such as RICE, Kano, OKRs, or opportunity cost only as a decision aid.
5. Explain tradeoffs, not just scores.
6. Mark which assumptions would change the recommendation.

## Output Contract

The final output should include: ranked or grouped recommendation, rationale, evidence and confidence, scoring table if useful, tradeoffs, dependencies, risks, deferred options, validation plan, and `Source note`.

If a requested roadmap action would modify a live roadmap or commitment, require explicit confirmation.
