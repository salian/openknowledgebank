# Product Metrics

Product metrics are source-specific. Do not invent formulas or trends. Ask for the metric glossary, analytics report, experiment readout, warehouse query, dashboard definition, or owner confirmation before treating a number as authoritative.

## Common Families

- Acquisition or awareness.
- Activation or onboarding.
- Engagement or usage.
- Retention or churn.
- Conversion or monetization.
- Satisfaction or qualitative feedback.
- Reliability, performance, or support burden.
- Experiment or feature adoption.

## Metric Review Checklist

- Metric definition and owner.
- Time period, time zone, refresh date, and attribution window.
- Segment, cohort, filters, and grain.
- Identity, deduplication, and aggregation logic.
- Known instrumentation or product changes.
- Confidence, caveats, and validation checks.
