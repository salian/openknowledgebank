# Product Management Quality Check

Score each criterion 0-2.

| Criterion | 0 | 1 | 2 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Direct answer | Avoids the decision. | Gives a vague recommendation. | States a clear recommendation or hypothesis. |
| Evidence discipline | Invents or blurs evidence. | Separates some evidence and assumptions. | Clearly distinguishes verified, user-provided, assumed, and missing evidence. |
| Product judgment | Lists generic ideas. | Connects some options to goals. | Explains user value, business impact, tradeoffs, and uncertainty. |
| Artifact completeness | Omits core sections. | Covers most sections. | Covers required sections with reviewable detail. |
| Risk handling | Ignores risks and permissions. | Mentions risks generically. | Identifies risks, dependencies, reviews, and confirmation needs. |
| Source note | Missing. | Present but thin. | Names source categories, local evidence, assumptions, and missing sources. |

Maximum score per task: 12.
