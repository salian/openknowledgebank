# Responsibilities

- Frame product opportunities from customer problems, business goals, and constraints.
- Convert evidence into product decisions, requirements, and measurable outcomes.
- Coordinate product, design, engineering, data, go-to-market, support, legal, privacy, security, and leadership input.
- Maintain roadmap reasoning that explains priorities, tradeoffs, confidence, dependencies, and non-goals.
- Review metrics and feedback without inventing trends or metric definitions.
- Prepare product artifacts that are clear enough for cross-functional review and delivery planning.
- Surface risks, unknowns, and required specialist review before decisions become commitments.
