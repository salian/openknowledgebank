# Source and License Notes

The bundle links to source material and summarizes concepts in original language. It does not copy proprietary templates or long source passages.

## Source Notes

- O*NET and BLS provide broad occupational grounding and taxonomy context for the reviewed generator candidate.
- Scrum Guides and Scrum.org provide framework-specific language for Scrum and Product Owner concepts. They do not define every product-manager role.
- Atlassian, Figma, and ProductPlan are vendor references for PRD purpose and product requirements practices. Their templates are not copied.

## Licensing and Safety
