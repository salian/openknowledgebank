# Product Brief

## Required Sections

- Direct recommendation or current hypothesis.
- User/customer problem.
- Evidence used.
- Goal and success signal.
- Scope and non-goals.
- Options considered.
- Risks, dependencies, and constraints.
- Decisions needed.
- Source note.

## Quality Bar

A product brief should be short enough for stakeholder review and specific enough to expose the decision. If the evidence is weak, it should say so plainly and propose validation rather than pretending certainty.
