# Product Requirements Document

## Required Sections

- Summary.
- Background and user problem.
- Goals and non-goals.
- Users, workflows, or segments.
- Scope.
- Requirements.
- Acceptance criteria.
- Measurement plan.
- Dependencies and constraints.
- Rollout or launch considerations.
- Risks and required reviews.
- Open questions and decision log.
- Source note.

## Quality Bar

The PRD should make the product decision reviewable. It should not smuggle in unverified implementation detail, analytics definitions, launch dates, legal/privacy/security assumptions, or customer commitments.
