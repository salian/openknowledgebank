# Roadmap Narrative

## Required Sections

- Planning horizon and decision context.
- Goals and constraints.
- Recommended sequence or grouping.
- Evidence and confidence by item.
- Tradeoffs and deferred work.
- Dependencies and capacity notes.
- Risks and review needs.
- What would change the recommendation.
- Source note.

## Quality Bar

A roadmap narrative should clarify intent without overstating certainty. Label dates, themes, or sequenced work according to the user's source of record: committed, planned, tentative, exploratory, or illustrative.
