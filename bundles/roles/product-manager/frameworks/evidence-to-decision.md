# Evidence to Decision

Use this frame when a product answer risks mixing facts, assumptions, opinions, and recommendations.

| Layer | Question | Output |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Source | Where did this claim come from? | Verified source, user-provided evidence, assumption, or missing evidence. |
| Observation | What happened or was said? | Neutral fact summary. |
| Interpretation | What might it mean? | Hypotheses and confidence. |
| Decision | What should we do now? | Recommendation, owner, next step, or evidence needed. |
| Risk | What could make this wrong? | Caveats, dependency, or review need. |

Do not collapse interpretation into observation. If the user asks for a decision with insufficient evidence, provide the best current hypothesis and list the missing evidence needed before commitment.
