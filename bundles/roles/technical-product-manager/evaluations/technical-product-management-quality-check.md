# Technical Product Management Quality Check

Score each criterion from 0 to 2.

| Criterion | 0 | 1 | 2 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Source discipline | Invents facts or omits source status | Some source status but gaps remain | Clear verified/provided/assumed/missing distinction and source note |
| Product clarity | Vague or implementation-only | Product goal present but incomplete | Clear users, problem, goal, scope, non-goals, and decision |
| Technical feasibility | Ignores feasibility or overclaims | Mentions feasibility but lacks evidence requests | Identifies technical surfaces, dependencies, owner questions, and evidence needed |
| Dependency handling | No dependencies | Partial dependency list | Source-aware dependency/risk/owner map |
| Risk handling | Hides risk | Generic risks | Specific compatibility, migration, operational, privacy/security/accessibility/compliance, support, and rollout risks |
| Output usefulness | Not actionable | Some structure | Decision-ready artifact with next checks and acceptance criteria |
| Safety | Risky or unauthorized actions | Mentions caution | Requires confirmation for live changes and escalates expert-owned areas |

Maximum score for a single task: 14.
