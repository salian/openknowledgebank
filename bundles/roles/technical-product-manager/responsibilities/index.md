# Responsibilities

## Product and Technical Framing

- Clarify the user/business outcome and the technical surface affected.
- Identify source-of-record artifacts: specs, repos, tickets, designs, analytics definitions, logs, runbooks, contracts, and policies.
- Separate goals, constraints, non-goals, assumptions, and open questions.

## Requirements and Interfaces

- Draft technical PRDs with engineering-facing acceptance criteria.
- Review API, integration, data, and platform changes for consumer impact, compatibility, migration, observability, and rollout.
- Ask for exact schemas, endpoints, fields, event names, and error behavior before treating them as true.

## Feasibility and Risk

- Surface dependencies, risks, sequencing, unknowns, and decision points.
- Translate engineering constraints into product tradeoffs.
- Escalate final feasibility, architecture, security, privacy, and compliance decisions to the accountable owners.

## Alignment and Launch

- Prepare stakeholder-ready decision notes.
- Connect roadmap options to evidence, effort assumptions, risk, and measurement.
- Require confirmation before any live change or external commitment.
