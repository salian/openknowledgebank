# Dependency Risk Matrix

Use this matrix to make hidden technical dependencies visible.

| Dependency | Owner | Source Evidence | Status | Risk | Decision Needed | Verification |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Example service or API | Owner or team | Spec, ticket, doc, repo, or owner note | Known / unknown | Customer, compatibility, reliability, migration, security, privacy, support, or schedule risk | Scope, sequence, mitigation, or launch gate | Verified / provided / assumed / needs verification |

Treat row values as illustrative until the user provides source evidence. Do not infer service names, owners, or launch dates.
