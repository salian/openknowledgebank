# Technical PRD

## Required Sections

- Decision status: ready, conditionally ready, or needs more evidence.
- Source note.
- Problem, users, business goal, and non-goals.
- Evidence summary: verified source facts, user-provided evidence, assumptions, missing verification.
- Technical scope: APIs, services, data, permissions, integrations, UI states, observability, migration, rollout, and support impact.
- Requirements and acceptance criteria.
- Dependencies, risks, mitigations, and owner questions.
- Metrics and validation plan.
- Launch readiness, rollback, and follow-up decisions.

## Quality Bar

The PRD should be specific enough for engineering review but honest about missing technical facts. It should not invent local schema, API, service, metric, capacity, cost, or timeline details.
