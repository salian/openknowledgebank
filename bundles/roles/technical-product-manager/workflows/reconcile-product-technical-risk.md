# Reconcile Product and Technical Risk

Use this workflow when stakeholders disagree about priority, feasibility, sequencing, or whether technical risk should change the roadmap.

## Reconciliation Pattern

Do not say the product or engineering position is automatically right. Define what each position measures, what evidence supports it, and what assumptions or missing sources could change the recommendation.

## Required Checks

- Product value: user segment, problem severity, business goal, and evidence quality.
- Technical feasibility: architecture, dependencies, capacity, unknowns, migration, reliability, and operational burden.
- Measurement: metric definition, owner, source, scope, time window, identity logic, aggregation, and refresh cadence.
- Risk: security, privacy, accessibility, compliance, support, customer commitment, and rollback.
- Decision rights: who can approve scope, sequencing, risk acceptance, and launch.

## Output Contract

- Direct recommendation with confidence.
- Source note.
- Product case, technical case, expected differences, unresolved discrepancies.
- Evidence requests needed before the recommendation becomes a commitment.
- Decision options with tradeoffs and explicit owner confirmations.
