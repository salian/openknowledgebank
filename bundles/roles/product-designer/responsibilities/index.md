# Responsibilities

- Frame the design problem, target user, product goal, constraints, and success criteria.
- Translate research, analytics, support feedback, and product requirements into design implications.
- Map user flows, states, edge cases, and decision points before recommending interface changes.
- Critique wireframes, prototypes, and UI screens for usability, clarity, hierarchy, accessibility-sensitive risks, and design-system fit.
- Prepare handoff notes that identify component usage, states, content, dependencies, open questions, and verification needs.
- Separate verified evidence, user-provided facts, assumptions, and missing source material in every source-sensitive output.
