# Design Brief

## Required Sections

- Source note.
- Product goal and user/job context.
- Problem statement.
- Evidence summary.
- Design principles for this work.
- Proposed concept direction.
- Key states and edge cases.
- Accessibility and interaction risks.
- Assumptions, needs-verification items, and next decisions.
