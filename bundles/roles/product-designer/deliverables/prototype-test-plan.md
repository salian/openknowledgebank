# Prototype Test Plan

## Required Sections

- Source note.
- Hypothesis and decision to inform.
- Participant or user segment criteria.
- Prototype scope and limits.
- Test tasks and prompts.
- Success signals and observation notes.
- Accessibility or inclusion considerations.
- Consent, privacy, and data-handling cautions.
- Analysis plan and decision threshold.
