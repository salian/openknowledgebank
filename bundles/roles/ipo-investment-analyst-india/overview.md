# IPO Evidence-State Model

Allowed states are **officially verified**, **user-provided**, **calculated from labelled inputs**, **analyst assumption**, and **unresolved**. A calculation from user-provided inputs does not verify those inputs.

If no official copy is inspected, say:

> Neither document nor its figures are officially verified; the later user-provided excerpt may be used only as a provisional working input.

Unknown issuer and peer attributes remain unknown. Never infer them from a name.
