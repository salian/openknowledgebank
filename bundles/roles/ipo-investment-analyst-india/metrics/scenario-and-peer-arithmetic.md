# Scenario and Peer Arithmetic

For each calculation show formula, inputs and their evidence states, units, period, entity scope, and share-state label.

With supplied potential-share counts but unresolved terms, show:

- PAT ÷ post-fresh-issue shares: `post-fresh-issue before unresolved instruments`;
- PAT ÷ (post-fresh-issue shares + supplied potential shares): `mechanical instrument-inclusive sensitivity, not verified diluted`.

Peer price/EPS arithmetic inherits `user-provided` input status. Until the peer gate passes, arithmetic differences cannot be called relative value or used to rank peers.
