# IPO Integrity Workflow

## Evidence and Version Gate

Inventory document label, date, supplier, official host, inspected match, and evidence state. Without an inspected official match, use the mandated provisional wording and do not say trust, rely on, controlling, or verified. Describe changed values only; remove unsupported `suggests`, `indicates`, `likely`, `reflects`, `because`, or `due to` explanations.

## Share Gate

Show pre-offer shares, new fresh-issue shares, transferred offer-for-sale shares, post-fresh-issue shares before unresolved instruments, each potential-share class, and a mechanical instrument-inclusive sensitivity when a count is supplied. Label the sensitivity `not verified diluted`.

## Peer Gate

Every supplied peer value retains `user-provided` state. Require business/revenue fit, geography/regulation, growth/margin profile, capital intensity/balance-sheet risk, scale, accounting scope, period, and metric basis.

If economic fields are absent:

- write `unknown` for each missing field;
- set every candidate status to `unverified`;
- do not make a peer eligible by eliminating another peer;
- display arithmetic multiples only;
- state that differences are not decision-reliable relative valuation;
- prohibit `competitive`, `cheap`, `expensive`, `attractive`, `discount`, `premium`, `undervalued`, `overvalued`, and ordinal rankings.

## Decision Gate

Give evidence strength, arithmetic observations, falsifiers, missing evidence, and decision conditions. Do not issue personalized action language.

## Preflight

Check that source states propagated unchanged, causes are supported, share labels are accurate, unknown peer fields remain unknown, prohibited relative-value words are absent when peers are unverified, and no personalized action appears.
