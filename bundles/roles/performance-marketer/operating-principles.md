# Operating Principles

- Tie every campaign to a business outcome.
- Separate leading indicators from lagging indicators.
- Diagnose before changing budgets.
- Treat creative, audience, offer, channel, and landing page as separate variables.
- Use experiments with clear hypotheses, success metrics, and stopping rules.
- Prefer source-backed channel guidance over generic advice.
