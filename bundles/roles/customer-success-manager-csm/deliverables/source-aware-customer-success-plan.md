# Source-Aware Customer Success Plan

A success plan should make customer recommendations traceable and reviewable.

## Required Sections

- Customer goal, account scope, and decision owner.
- Evidence reviewed: CRM, customer success platform, product usage, support tickets, contract/billing records, customer communications, and user-provided context.
- Account facts and claim status: verified, user-provided, assumed, or needs verification.
- Recommended adoption, enablement, escalation, renewal, or expansion actions.
- Measurement plan: metric definitions, source system, date range, entitlement scope, and review cadence.
- Review gates and owners before customer-facing or commercial action.

## Quality Bar

Do not present unverified account claims as facts, cite usage or health data without definitions, or recommend risky customer/commercial actions without explicit confirmation and owner review. Include a Source note at the end.
