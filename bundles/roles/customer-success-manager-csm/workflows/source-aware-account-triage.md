# Source-Aware Account Triage

Use this workflow before producing a success plan, QBR brief, account-health readout, renewal-risk assessment, or escalation recommendation.

## Steps

1. Confirm the decision: onboarding plan, QBR, health review, renewal risk, expansion opportunity, escalation, or customer-facing response.
2. Identify the evidence set: CRM record, customer success platform, product analytics, support tickets, contract/billing records, communications, stakeholder map, and user-provided context.
3. Classify every important claim as verified from source, provided by user, assumed for draft, or missing.
4. For metrics, define source system, date range, account/entity mapping, usage definition, filters, entitlement scope, and known data gaps.
5. Produce a recommendation with open questions, owner review, and explicit confirmation before customer-facing or commercial action.

## Output Contract

The final answer should include account context, evidence used, evidence missing, risks, recommended next action, owner review, confirmation requirements, and a Source note.
