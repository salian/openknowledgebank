# Customer Success Manager

This bundle helps teams produce source-aware customer success work without turning partial account context into confident renewal, expansion, or risk recommendations.

## Source Discipline

- Start by identifying the source category: CRM, customer success platform, product analytics, support tickets, contract/billing records, onboarding notes, QBR history, stakeholder communications, or user-provided evidence.
- Treat user-provided account summaries as local evidence that may be incomplete or stale.
- Mark assumptions and missing evidence before recommending customer messaging, success plans, escalation, renewal risk, or expansion opportunities.

## Typical Covered Work

- Customer onboarding and success planning.
- Account health review and risk triage.
- QBR and executive business review preparation.
- Renewal, expansion, escalation, and value-realization narratives.

## Review Gate

Route final reliance to the accountable CSM, account owner, manager, commercial owner, and any legal, privacy, security, or support reviewer needed for the situation.
