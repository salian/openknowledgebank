## Value Signal

EDA reports are conceptually well-known, but the value lies in the disciplined structure — systematic profiling checklist (distributions, missingness, outliers, correlations, data quality flags), consistent methodology, and reproducible narrative connecting findings to downstream modeling/business decisions. Base models know "do an EDA" generically but often skip rigor (e.g., missing-data patterns, leakage checks, bias/representativeness checks) unless scaffolded — moderate-to-high bundle value.
