Produced across 3 distinct roles (support manager, CX analyst, API product manager) spanning both customer support and platform/API contexts, indicating broad applicability across service-oriented functions (support, IT ops, vendor management, platform reliability) — giving it solid hub value. It's not deeply regulated like legal/financial artifacts, but SLA adherence reporting is a standard operational governance requirement (contractual, vendor, and internal ops), which nudges it toward P1 rather than P2.
