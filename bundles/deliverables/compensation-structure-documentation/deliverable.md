## Value Signal

Compensation structure and pay band documentation is a structurally-nuanced, increasingly regulated artifact (pay transparency laws like NY, CA, CO, EU Pay Transparency Directive require documented, defensible pay bands/ranges). The base model knows the general concept of pay bands but not the disciplined structure needed: job leveling frameworks, market pricing methodology, band width/midpoint/compa-ratio logic, internal equity analysis, geographic differentials, and audit-defensibility documentation. While produced directly by only one specialist role, it is a foundational HR artifact that many other roles (HRBPs, recruiters, people managers, finance/HRIS) reference and depend on, giving it above-average hub value despite narrow production ownership.
