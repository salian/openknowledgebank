## Value Signal

Moderate-high bundle value. Org design proposals are conceptually familiar to a base model, but a disciplined version requires structurally nuanced content the model doesn't natively produce well: current-vs-proposed org charts, span-of-control analysis, RACI/decision-rights shifts, headcount/cost impact, transition and communication plans, and risk mitigation for morale/attrition. That checklist-level rigor (not just 'draw new boxes') is where a curated artifact adds real value over a generic prompt.
