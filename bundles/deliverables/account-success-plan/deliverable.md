## Value Signal

Account plans are common, well-known documents in general business/sales knowledge, but a genuinely useful CSM account plan requires disciplined structure: stakeholder mapping/org chart, health score and risk indicators, whitespace/expansion analysis, renewal timeline, success plan tied to customer's business outcomes, executive relationship map, and QBR cadence. Base model knows the generic concept ("account plan") but not the CS-specific rigor around health scoring, expansion whitespace, and outcome-mapping — so there is moderate bundle value from the structured checklist, though it's less regulatory/structurally-nuanced than compliance or legal artifacts.
