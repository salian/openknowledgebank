## Value Signal

Budget and financial forecasts are widely known documents at a conceptual level, but a properly built one requires structural discipline: revenue/expense driver modeling, scenario assumptions (best/base/worst case), variance tracking against actuals, capital allocation, and cross-departmental input reconciliation. Base models know the general concept of "a budget" but not the disciplined forecasting methodology (rolling forecasts, driver-based modeling, sensitivity analysis, assumptions documentation) that finance teams actually use.
