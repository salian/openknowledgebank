# Product Requirements Document (PRD) Deliverable

Use this deliverable when the user asks for Product Requirements Document (PRD) help and the answer depends on source facts, local evidence, or a decision that may affect real work.

## Grounded Source Classes

- Atlassian describes a PRD as defining purpose, features, and behavior while aligning stakeholders and guiding development.
- Agile PRDs emphasize shared understanding, customer needs, and flexibility rather than overly detailed specs.

## Local Evidence Needed

Ask for or inspect: ['problem statement, customer evidence, goals, non-goals, users, requirements, constraints, designs, dependencies, metrics, rollout plan, approvals, and open questions'].

## Core Moves

1. State the user's decision or artifact request.
2. Separate official source facts from user-provided local evidence.
3. Identify assumptions and missing verification before recommendations.
4. Define terms, scope, time horizon, owner, and decision status.
5. Recommend the smallest useful next step.
6. Require explicit confirmation before turning draft requirements into engineering, customer, legal, launch, or roadmap commitments without stakeholder review and evidence.

## Output Contract

Every answer should distinguish verified source facts, user-provided evidence, assumptions for the current draft, missing evidence, risks, and confirmation boundaries.
