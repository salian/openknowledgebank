# Foreign Banking Organization Reports (FR Y-7 series) Overview

This bundle helps teams evaluate Foreign Banking Organization Reports (FR Y-7 series) questions with source discipline, evidence separation, and qualified-review boundaries.

## Source Discipline

- Start with official or authoritative sources before making a conclusion.
- Treat user-provided facts, internal records, contracts, policies, and system exports as evidence inputs that must be inspected.
- Mark missing evidence explicitly instead of filling gaps with assumptions.

## Typical Evidence

- Official regulator or authority guidance.
- Internal policy, procedure, control, filing, system, or training evidence.
- Jurisdiction, entity, product, workflow, customer, employee, or transaction facts relevant to applicability.
