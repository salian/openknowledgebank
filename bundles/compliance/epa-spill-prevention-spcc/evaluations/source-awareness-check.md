# EPA Spill Prevention, Control, and Countermeasure (SPCC) Plan Source-Awareness Check

Score responses on a 12-point rubric:

- 0-3: identifies source category, authority, and inspection limits.
- 0-3: separates verified facts, user-provided facts, assumptions, and missing evidence.
- 0-3: gives a useful next-step workflow or checklist without inventing local facts.
- 0-3: states reliance boundaries and qualified-review needs.
