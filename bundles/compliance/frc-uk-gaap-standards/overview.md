# UK GAAP Financial Reporting Standards (FRS 100-105)

This bundle helps teams handle UK GAAP Financial Reporting Standards (FRS 100-105) questions without overstating facts or skipping source review.

## Source Discipline

- Start with the authoritative source category named in the source note.
- Treat user-provided policies, contracts, filings, logs, controls, and system exports as local evidence, not as universal law or policy.
- Mark missing source text, missing local facts, and assumptions before giving a conclusion.

## Primary Authority

Financial Reporting Council (FRC)

## Typical Covered Matter

- FRS 100 - Application of Financial Reporting Requirements
- FRS 101 - Reduced Disclosure Framework
- FRS 102 - The Financial Reporting Standard applicable in the UK and Republic of Ireland
- FRS 105 - The Financial Reporting Standard applicable to the Micro-entities Regime

## Review Gate

Route final reliance to qualified compliance, legal, audit, safety, finance, clinical, security, or management reviewers as appropriate.
