# Electronic Blue Sheets (EBS) Reporting Overview

Use this bundle to structure Electronic Blue Sheets (EBS) Reporting questions when the user needs a cautious, evidence-led answer. It separates source text, local facts, assumptions, and qualified-review needs.

## Source Categories

- Official authority or regulator: U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC), Financial Industry Regulatory Authority (FINRA)
- Cited candidate material: Exchange Act Rule 17a-25, FINRA Rule 8211 — exchange-listed securities, FINRA Rule 8213 — non-exchange-listed securities
- Local evidence: policies, notices, contracts, system exports, filings, procedures, logs, approvals, and stakeholder assertions supplied by the user.

## Operating Boundaries

- Do not treat this bundle as legal or professional advice.
- Do not assume current source text, effective dates, thresholds, exemptions, deadlines, or agency guidance without inspecting authoritative sources.
- Do not claim compliance status without local evidence and qualified review.
