# Federal Trade Commission Act, Section 5 — Unfair or Deceptive Acts or Practices

This bundle helps teams handle Federal Trade Commission Act, Section 5 — Unfair or Deceptive Acts or Practices questions without overstating facts or skipping source review.

## Source Discipline

- Start with the authoritative source category named in the source note.
- Treat user-provided policies, contracts, filings, logs, controls, and system exports as local evidence, not as universal law or policy.
- Mark missing source text, missing local facts, and assumptions before giving a conclusion.

## Primary Authority

Federal Trade Commission (FTC)

## Typical Covered Matter

- Section 5 UDAP baseline prohibition
- Dark Patterns Enforcement Policy Statement (2021)
- Bringing Dark Patterns to Light Staff Report (2022)
- Policy Statement on Biometric Information and Section 5
- Data Breach Response: A Guide for Business (guidance)
- Standard Consent Order Compliance Reporting (biennial independent assessments, 20-year monitoring)

## Review Gate

Route final reliance to qualified compliance, legal, audit, safety, finance, clinical, security, or management reviewers as appropriate.
