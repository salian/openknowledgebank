# BC PIPA Access and Correction Rights (PIPA ss. 23-24, 28-29, 31-32) Source-Aware Brief

## Direct Answer

State what can be said now and whether the answer is verified, provisional, or blocked by missing evidence.

## Source Note

Name the official or authoritative source category, source inspected or still needed, local evidence, and missing facts.

## Evidence Matrix

| Claim | Status | Source or Evidence | Caveat |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Source requirement or role standard | verified / needs verification | Official source, authoritative source, or missing source | Cite only inspected sources. |
| Local fact | provided / missing | User evidence | Do not infer facts. |
| Draft assumption | assumed | Stated assumption | Do not treat as conclusion. |
| Action | needs review | Qualified review and explicit confirmation | Do not act without confirmation. |

## Review Focus

Right of access / duty to produce on request (s. 23), Correction of personal information (s. 24), Duty to respond to an access request (s. 28), 30-day time limit to respond (s. 29), Extension of time limit and notice (s. 31), Fees and written fee estimate (s. 32).
