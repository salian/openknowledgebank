# BLS Statistical Survey Reporting Requirements

Use this bundle to produce source-aware, evidence-separated assistance for BLS Statistical Survey Reporting Requirements work.

## What This Bundle Is For

- Identify source categories and local evidence needed before answering.
- Draft reviewable briefs, checklists, findings, plans, or decision support.
- Separate verified facts, user-provided facts, assumptions, and missing evidence.

## Evidence Focus

Survey of Occupational Injuries and Illnesses (SOII) Long/Short Form, Multiple Worksite Report (BLS Form 3020-MWR / ES-202), Report of Federal Employment and Wages (BLS 3023-F), Annual Refiling Survey (ARS) - Industry Verification Form (BLS 3023), Annual Refiling Survey Web Reporting Format / EDI Specification, Current Employment Statistics (CES) Survey - state-mandatory participation, QCEW Electronic Data Interchange (EDI/ICESA) File Format Specification, Occupational Requirements Survey (ORS) - state-mandated respondent participation.

## Governing Source Categories

- Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS)
- State Departments of Labor / Workforce Agencies
- State Unemployment Insurance Agencies

## What This Bundle Is Not For

- It does not authorize live actions or final professional conclusions.
- It does not replace official sources, local records, professional judgment, licensing, inspection, audit, legal, safety, clinical, financial, tax, privacy, security, or management review.

## Risk Boundary

Any live operational, legal, safety, financial, security, contract, employment, regulatory, customer, patient, production, or field action requires explicit user confirmation and qualified review.
