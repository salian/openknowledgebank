# Sanitation SOPs and Sanitation Requirements (9 CFR Part 416)

This bundle helps teams handle Sanitation SOPs and Sanitation Requirements (9 CFR Part 416) questions without overstating facts or skipping source review.

## Source Discipline

- Start with the authoritative source category named in the source note.
- Treat user-provided policies, contracts, filings, logs, controls, and system exports as local evidence, not as universal law or policy.
- Mark missing source text, missing local facts, and assumptions before giving a conclusion.

## Primary Authority

USDA Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS)

## Typical Covered Matter

- Written Sanitation Standard Operating Procedures (9 CFR Part 416)
- Sanitation SOP Daily Recordkeeping (9 CFR 416.16)
- Sanitation Facility and Operating Requirements (9 CFR 416.1-416.6)

## Review Gate

Route final reliance to qualified compliance, legal, audit, safety, finance, clinical, security, or management reviewers as appropriate.
