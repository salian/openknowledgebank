# Business Opportunity Rule

Use this bundle to produce source-aware, evidence-separated assistance for Business Opportunity Rule work.

## What This Bundle Is For

- Identify source categories and local evidence needed before answering.
- Draft reviewable briefs, checklists, findings, plans, or decision support.
- Separate verified facts, user-provided facts, assumptions, and missing evidence.

## Evidence Focus

16 C.F.R. Part 437.

## Governing Source Categories

- Federal Trade Commission (FTC)

## What This Bundle Is Not For

- It does not authorize live actions or final professional conclusions.
- It does not replace official sources, local records, professional judgment, licensing, inspection, audit, legal, safety, clinical, financial, tax, privacy, security, or management review.

## Risk Boundary

Any live operational, legal, safety, financial, security, contract, employment, regulatory, customer, patient, production, or field action requires explicit user confirmation and qualified review.
