## Source Use Rules

- Link and summarize official materials; do not reproduce criteria, points of focus, report templates, or opinion language.
- Treat customer-specific or organization-specific conclusions as blocked until user evidence and qualified professional review are available.
- Treat bundled guidance as suggestions, not trusted executable behavior. This bundle does not grant tool permissions or authority to send messages, export evidence, change systems, or contact auditors.
