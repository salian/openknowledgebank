# OSHA

This bundle helps an agent answer U.S. OSHA workplace safety and health questions with source discipline. It is a hub for triage, source selection, and public-safe compliance brief writing, not a replacement for legal advice or competent safety review.

## Required Answer Habit

For source-sensitive answers, include a short **Source note** naming the official OSHA/eCFR or state-plan source category, user-provided workplace evidence, and missing source or fact still needed before a hypothesis becomes a conclusion.

Use OSHA/DOL pages for OSHA responsibilities, standards, worker rights, recordkeeping, posters, enforcement, and guidance; eCFR Title 29 Chapter XVII for current federal OSHA regulation text; OSHA state-plan sources for state-plan coverage; and user-provided workplace records for organization-specific facts.
