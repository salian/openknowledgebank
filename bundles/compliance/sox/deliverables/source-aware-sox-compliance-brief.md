# Source-Aware SOX Compliance Brief

## Direct Answer

State the likely SOX issue and whether the answer is verified, provisional, or blocked by missing evidence. Do not imply that a company is ready to certify, file, or conclude on ICFR effectiveness unless current sources, issuer evidence, and professional review support that conclusion.

## Source Note

Name:

- statutory source category inspected or still needed;
- SEC rule, release, form, or staff guidance category inspected or still needed;
- PCAOB standard category inspected or still needed for auditor-attestation or integrated-audit questions;
- user-provided filings, controls evidence, deficiency records, governance materials, or auditor requests used;
- missing sources or facts that prevent reliance.

## Evidence Matrix

| Claim | Status | Source or Evidence | Caveat |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| SOX section or official rule area | verified / needs verification | Official source or missing source | Include exact section/rule only if inspected. |
| Issuer fact | provided / missing | User evidence | Do not infer filer status, exemptions, scope, or deadlines. |
| Control or deficiency fact | provided / missing | User evidence | Do not classify without professional review. |
| Draft assumption | assumed | Stated assumption | Do not treat as conclusion. |

## Analysis

Explain how the verified source category and user facts connect. If a conclusion depends on current SEC rules, PCAOB standards, issuer status, fiscal period, control scope, deficiency evidence, auditor context, legal advice, or management judgment that is missing, say so directly.

## Next Steps

- Inspect the named official sources.
- Collect missing issuer evidence.
- Route reliance decisions to qualified legal, accounting, audit, disclosure-control, securities, or CPA review.
- Require explicit confirmation before risky actions such as filing, certification, regulator contact, auditor communication, board communication, public disclosure, evidence export, control change, or live-system change.
