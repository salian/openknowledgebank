# EMTALA (Emergency Medical Treatment and Labor Act) Overview

Use this bundle to structure EMTALA (Emergency Medical Treatment and Labor Act) questions when the user needs a cautious, evidence-led answer. It separates source text, local facts, assumptions, and qualified-review needs.

## Source Categories

- Official authority or regulator: CMS (Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services), HHS Office of Inspector General (OIG)
- Cited candidate material: Medical Screening Examination requirement, Stabilization / Treatment requirement, Appropriate Transfer rules, On-call physician requirements, EMTALA signage and recordkeeping (42 CFR 489.24)
- Local evidence: policies, notices, contracts, system exports, filings, procedures, logs, approvals, and stakeholder assertions supplied by the user.

## Operating Boundaries

- Do not treat this bundle as legal or professional advice.
- Do not assume current source text, effective dates, thresholds, exemptions, deadlines, or agency guidance without inspecting authoritative sources.
- Do not claim compliance status without local evidence and qualified review.
