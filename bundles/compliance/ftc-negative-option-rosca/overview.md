# FTC Negative Option Rule (16 CFR Part 425) and Restore Online Shoppers' Confidence Act (ROSCA)

This bundle helps teams handle FTC Negative Option Rule (16 CFR Part 425) and Restore Online Shoppers' Confidence Act (ROSCA) questions without overstating facts or skipping source review.

## Source Discipline

- Start with the authoritative source category named in the source note.
- Treat user-provided policies, contracts, filings, logs, controls, and system exports as local evidence, not as universal law or policy.
- Mark missing source text, missing local facts, and assumptions before giving a conclusion.

## Primary Authority

Federal Trade Commission (FTC)

## Typical Covered Matter

- 16 CFR Part 425 (original 1973 rule, post-2024-vacatur recodification)
- March 2026 ANPRM to revive click-to-cancel provisions
- Restore Online Shoppers' Confidence Act (ROSCA)

## Review Gate

Route final reliance to qualified compliance, legal, audit, safety, finance, clinical, security, or management reviewers as appropriate.
