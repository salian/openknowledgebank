# References

## Official Source Categories

- ADA.gov and DOJ Civil Rights Division pages for ADA overview, Title III public accommodation, and web accessibility guidance.
- Current ADA statute and DOJ regulations for exact legal text and definitions.
- DOJ Title II web/mobile accessibility rule materials when the subject is a state or local government public entity.
- W3C/WAI WCAG pages for technical standard, conformance, success-criterion, and version facts.
- User-provided business, website, app, audit, manual test, assistive-technology, code, design, screenshot, policy, vendor, accessibility statement, legal communication, and remediation evidence for organization-specific facts.

## Source Discipline

Do not treat this bundle as a substitute for current official sources. For exact legal text, case-law rules, state-law requirements, technical success-criterion wording, compliance deadlines, remediation obligations, accessibility statements, or product conformance claims, inspect the current source and require qualified professional review before reliance.
