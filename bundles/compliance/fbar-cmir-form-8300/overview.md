# FBAR, CMIR, and Form 8300 Reporting Overview

Use this bundle to structure FBAR, CMIR, and Form 8300 Reporting questions when the user needs a cautious, evidence-led answer. It separates source text, local facts, assumptions, and qualified-review needs.

## Source Categories

- Official authority or regulator: Financial Crimes Enforcement Network (FinCEN)
- Cited candidate material: FinCEN Form 114 FBAR (31 CFR 1010.350), FinCEN Form 105 CMIR (31 CFR 1010.340), FinCEN/IRS Form 8300 (31 CFR 1010.330)
- Local evidence: policies, notices, contracts, system exports, filings, procedures, logs, approvals, and stakeholder assertions supplied by the user.
