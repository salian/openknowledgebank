# FDA Medical Device Reporting (MDR/eMDR) Overview

Use this bundle to structure FDA Medical Device Reporting (MDR/eMDR) questions when the user needs a cautious, evidence-led answer. It separates source text, local facts, assumptions, and qualified-review needs.

## Source Categories

- Official authority or regulator: Food and Drug Administration (FDA)
- Cited candidate material: Medical Device Reporting (MDR) — 21 CFR Part 803, Electronic Medical Device Reporting (eMDR) — HL7 ICSR XML format, MedWatch Form FDA 3500A (Mandatory Reporting Form)
- Local evidence: policies, notices, contracts, system exports, filings, procedures, logs, approvals, and stakeholder assertions supplied by the user.
