# Pathogen Reduction Performance Standards and Process-Control Testing (9 CFR 310.25; Directive 10,250.1)

This bundle helps teams handle Pathogen Reduction Performance Standards and Process-Control Testing (9 CFR 310.25; Directive 10,250.1) questions without overstating facts or skipping source review.

## Source Discipline

- Start with the authoritative source category named in the source note.
- Treat user-provided policies, contracts, filings, logs, controls, and system exports as local evidence, not as universal law or policy.
- Mark missing source text, missing local facts, and assumptions before giving a conclusion.

## Primary Authority

USDA Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS)

## Typical Covered Matter

- Generic E. coli Biotype 1 Process-Control Testing (9 CFR 310.25(a))
- Salmonella Pathogen Reduction Performance Standards (9 CFR 310.25(b))
- Salmonella and Campylobacter Verification Performance Standards for Raw Poultry (Directive 10,250.1)

## Review Gate

Route final reliance to qualified compliance, legal, audit, safety, finance, clinical, security, or management reviewers as appropriate.
