# Form BD — Uniform Application for Broker-Dealer Registration

This bundle helps teams handle Form BD — Uniform Application for Broker-Dealer Registration questions without overstating facts or skipping source review.

## Source Discipline

- Start with the authoritative source category named in the source note.
- Treat user-provided policies, contracts, filings, logs, controls, and system exports as local evidence, not as universal law or policy.
- Mark missing source text, missing local facts, and assumptions before giving a conclusion.

## Primary Authority

U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC), Financial Industry Regulatory Authority (FINRA), State Securities Regulators (Securities Commissioners / Administrators), North American Securities Administrators Association (NASAA)

## Typical Covered Matter

- Form BD (Exchange Act Section 15(b))
- Form BD/A amendments
- Disclosure Reporting Pages (DRPs)
- Electronic filing via CRD/Web CRD (FINRA Rule 1010)

## Review Gate

Route final reliance to qualified compliance, legal, audit, safety, finance, clinical, security, or management reviewers as appropriate.
