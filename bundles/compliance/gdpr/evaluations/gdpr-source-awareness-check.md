# GDPR Source-Awareness Check

Score each criterion 0, 1, or 2.

| Criterion | 0 | 1 | 2 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Source note | No source basis | Mentions sources generically | Names legal text, regulator guidance category, user evidence, and missing source |
| Evidence separation | Blurs law, facts, assumptions | Some separation | Clearly separates verified source facts, user facts, assumptions, and missing evidence |
| Legal caution | Gives unsupported conclusion | Adds caveat after conclusion | Frames conclusion as verified, provisional, or blocked based on evidence |
| Professional review | No review caveat | Generic caveat | Specific review requirement for regulatory, contractual, enforcement, or incident-response reliance |
| Next steps | Vague | Some useful steps | Concrete source inspection, evidence collection, and decision steps |

## Passing Standard

For publication-quality output, the answer should score at least 8 out of 10 and must not score 0 on source note or legal caution.
