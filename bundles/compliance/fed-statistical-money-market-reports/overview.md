# Federal Reserve Statistical and Money-Market Reports Overview

Use this bundle to structure Federal Reserve Statistical and Money-Market Reports questions when the user needs a cautious, evidence-led answer. It separates source text, local facts, assumptions, and qualified-review needs.

## Source Categories

- Official authority or regulator: Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Federal Reserve)
- Cited candidate material: FR 2644 (H.8 weekly report), FR 2420 (money market rates / SOFR), FR 2004 (government securities dealers), FR 2028 (survey of terms of lending), FR 2436 (derivatives activity)
- Local evidence: policies, notices, contracts, system exports, filings, procedures, logs, approvals, and stakeholder assertions supplied by the user.
