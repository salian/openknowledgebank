# SEC Reporting & Disclosure

Use this bundle to produce source-aware, evidence-separated assistance for SEC reporting and disclosure work for US public companies and SEC-reporting issuers.

## What This Bundle Is For

- Identify the SEC source category and issuer evidence needed before answering.
- Draft reviewable briefs, checklists, findings, plans, or decision support.
- Separate verified SEC source facts, user-provided issuer facts, assumptions, and missing evidence.
- Route work across periodic reporting, current reporting, proxy and governance disclosure, beneficial ownership, executive compensation, insider trading policy disclosure, conflict minerals, cybersecurity disclosure, EDGAR, Inline XBRL, and Regulation FD.

## Evidence Focus

Current SEC forms, SEC rules and releases, SEC staff guidance where appropriate, EDGAR Filer Manual versions, Inline XBRL guidance, issuer filings, issuer status, fiscal period, security class, transaction or event facts, disclosure controls, board or committee materials, accounting/audit evidence, and counsel-reviewed records.

## Governing Source Categories

- U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission.
- Current SEC forms and instructions.
- EDGAR and Inline XBRL source material.
- Issuer-specific records and qualified reviewer evidence.

## What This Bundle Is Not For

- It does not authorize live filings, public disclosures, investor communications, trading-policy changes, or final professional conclusions.
- It does not replace official SEC sources, issuer records, disclosure controls, professional judgment, legal advice, accounting or audit review, securities-law analysis, tax review, governance review, or management approval.
- It does not cover non-SEC regulator obligations except as adjacent context.

## Risk Boundary

Any live filing, public disclosure, investor communication, financial reporting, trading-window, governance, regulatory, legal, accounting, audit, tax, security incident, data export, or policy action requires explicit user confirmation and qualified review.
