# SEC Disclosure Source-Aware Brief

## Direct Answer

State what can be said now and whether the answer is verified, provisional, or blocked by missing source or issuer evidence.

## Source Note

Name the official SEC source category, current source inspected or still needed, issuer evidence used, source date or version when relevant, and missing facts.

## Source Category

Identify the applicable category, such as periodic/current reporting, proxy/governance, beneficial ownership, Section 16, executive compensation, insider trading policy, conflict minerals, cybersecurity disclosure, EDGAR/Inline XBRL, Regulation FD, or adjacent/non-SEC.

## Evidence Matrix

| Claim | Status | Source or Evidence | Caveat |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| SEC source requirement or source category | verified / needs verification | Current SEC source or missing source | Cite only inspected sources. |
| Issuer fact | provided / missing | User evidence or issuer filing | Do not infer facts. |
| Draft assumption | assumed | Stated assumption | Do not treat as conclusion. |
| Action | needs review | Qualified review and explicit confirmation | Do not act without confirmation. |

## Review Focus

Check whether the answer requires current inspection of SEC forms, rules, staff guidance, EDGAR Filer Manual, Inline XBRL guidance, issuer filings, issuer status, fiscal period, security class, transaction or event facts, disclosure controls, board or committee records, accounting/audit evidence, or counsel-reviewed records.

## Risk Boundary

No filing, public disclosure, investor communication, policy change, trading-window action, EDGAR submission, data export, accounting conclusion, audit conclusion, tax conclusion, legal conclusion, governance action, or regulatory response should be treated as authorized without explicit user confirmation and qualified review.

## Next Steps

- Inspect the current official SEC source category.
- Collect minimum necessary issuer evidence.
- Reconcile source facts with issuer facts and disclosure controls.
- Route to qualified review.
- Require explicit confirmation before live action.
