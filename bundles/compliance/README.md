# Compliance Bundles

Compliance bundles describe rules, standards, regulations, reporting regimes, and validation requirements.

Examples:

- AVETMISS compliance
- SEBI investment adviser regulations
- GDPR data processing

Compliance bundles should cite official sources, record jurisdictions and authorities, include effective dates where relevant, and explain applicability boundaries.

They should not present themselves as legal, tax, financial, medical, or other professional advice.
