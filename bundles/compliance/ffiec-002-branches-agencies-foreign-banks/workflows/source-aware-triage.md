# Source-Aware Triage

1. Identify the decision: applicability, readiness, filing, disclosure, control, reporting, or operational claim.
2. Name the source category needed for Report of Assets and Liabilities of U.S. Branches and Agencies of Foreign Banks (FFIEC 002): official authority text, guidance, filing instructions, contract, internal policy, control evidence, or system record.
3. Separate verified source facts from user-provided facts, assumptions, and missing evidence.
4. Provide a provisional answer only when the evidence supports it.
5. Route final reliance to qualified review before operational, legal, financial, safety, employment, disclosure, or public action.
