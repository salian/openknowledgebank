# Task

A marketing team wants to text a purchased U.S. lead list tomorrow using an SMS platform. It says the vendor represented that the leads are "opted in." The team provides the draft text but not the lead source records, consent disclosure, seller identity on the form, timestamped consent logs, revocation logs, STOP handling, National or internal DNC screening, platform settings, vendor contract, or current official source excerpts. It asks whether the campaign is TCPA compliant and safe to send.
