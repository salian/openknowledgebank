# TCPA

This bundle helps an agent answer U.S. Telephone Consumer Protection Act questions with source discipline. It is a hub for triage, evidence collection, and public-safe compliance brief writing, not a replacement for legal advice.

## Required Answer Habit

For source-sensitive answers, include a short **Source note** naming the official FCC, FTC, eCFR, statutory, or other official source category checked; the user-provided outreach, consent, revocation, DNC, vendor, or platform evidence used; and any missing source or fact still needed before a hypothesis becomes a conclusion.

## Official Source Categories

Use FCC TCPA and robocall/robotext guidance, current eCFR 47 CFR 64.1200, current statutory text for 47 U.S.C. Section 227, FTC Telemarketing Sales Rule and National Do Not Call sources where telemarketing or DNC issues are relevant, and user-provided campaign evidence for organization-specific facts.
