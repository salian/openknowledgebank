# ECOA / Regulation B Overview

Use this bundle to structure ECOA / Regulation B questions when the user needs a cautious, evidence-led answer. It separates source text, local facts, assumptions, and qualified-review needs.

## Source Categories

- Official authority or regulator: Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB), State Mortgage / Financial Services Regulators
- Cited candidate material: Adverse action notification & retention (Reg B 1002.9), Appraisal & written valuation copies (Reg B 1002.14), Nondiscrimination & monitoring data collection (Reg B 1002.4, 1002.13)
- Local evidence: policies, notices, contracts, system exports, filings, procedures, logs, approvals, and stakeholder assertions supplied by the user.

## Operating Boundaries

- Do not treat this bundle as legal or professional advice.
- Do not assume current source text, effective dates, thresholds, exemptions, deadlines, or agency guidance without inspecting authoritative sources.
- Do not claim compliance status without local evidence and qualified review.
