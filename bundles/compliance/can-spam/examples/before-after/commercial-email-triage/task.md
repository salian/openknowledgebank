# Task

A marketing team wants to send a promotional email to a purchased U.S. prospect list tomorrow. It provides the draft body copy but not full headers, list provenance, opt-out history, footer address, unsubscribe-link behavior, ESP suppression settings, vendor contracts, or current source excerpts. It asks whether the campaign is CAN-SPAM compliant and safe to send.
