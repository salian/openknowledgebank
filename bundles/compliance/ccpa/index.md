# CCPA

This bundle helps an agent answer California Consumer Privacy Act questions with source discipline. It is a hub for triage, source selection, and public-safe compliance brief writing, not a replacement for legal advice.

## Required Answer Habit

For source-sensitive answers, include a short **Source note** naming the official statute or regulation source category, relevant California Attorney General or California Privacy Protection Agency material, user-provided evidence, and any missing source or fact still needed before a hypothesis becomes a conclusion.
