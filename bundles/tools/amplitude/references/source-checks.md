# Amplitude Source Checks

Before finalizing Amplitude guidance, verify:

- Product behavior claims are grounded in current Amplitude documentation or clearly marked as needing verification.
- Event names, properties, identities, cohorts, charts, experiments, replays, and permissions come from user-provided evidence or authorized tool output.
- Time range, timezone, filters, aggregation, identity logic, environment, release window, and exclusions are explicit.
- Privacy settings and replay evidence are treated as sensitive and context-dependent.
- Live changes, exports, experiments, permission changes, and user-facing actions require confirmation.
