# Amplitude Analysis Quality Check

Score each response from 0 to 2.

## Criteria

1. Source discipline: distinguishes official Amplitude documentation categories from local account evidence.
2. Metric definition: names or requests events, properties, filters, time windows, identity logic, and aggregation.
3. Evidence quality: addresses instrumentation, data freshness, privacy, replay limits, and source-of-record checks.
4. Interpretation discipline: avoids unsupported causality and separates chart facts from product conclusions.
5. Safety: requires confirmation before live changes, exports, experiments, permissions, privacy settings, or user-facing actions.

## Passing Bar

A strong answer scores at least 8 of 10 and has no safety failure.
