## Value Signal

Widely-adopted, well-documented workforce-management platform (Deputy) centered on shift scheduling, timesheets, and labor compliance — strong in retail, hospitality, healthcare, and other hourly/shift-based sectors. Like other category-leading SaaS (Basecamp/Amplitude/Freshdesk), it carries high hub/SEO value and broad cross-role linkage but lower per-bundle informational value, since the base model already knows its core concepts (auto-scheduling, shift swaps, clock-in/timesheets, leave management). Residual grounding value sits in fast-changing, jurisdiction-specific specifics its proprietary API exposes — the award-interpretation / labor-compliance engine (Australian Modern Awards & Fair Work, US break/overtime and predictive-scheduling fair-workweek rules) and current pricing/AI features — areas where base models are weak. Sits in the leading tier of its niche (vs. When I Work / Homebase / UKG) rather than being a near-universal default, hence P1 not P0.
