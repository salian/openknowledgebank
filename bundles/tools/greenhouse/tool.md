Greenhouse is the category-leading structured-hiring ATS for mid-to-large companies, with strong hub/SEO value: many recruiting, talent-acquisition, people-ops/HR, and hiring-manager role bundles would plausibly link to it (sourcing, structured interviews/scorecards, offer workflows, DE&I/EEOC reporting). Large integration-partner ecosystem reinforces hub value. Confined to the hiring function rather than universally horizontal, so it lands just short of P0 ubiquity (Salesforce/Slack/HubSpot) — same P1 tier as Asana and Apollo.io.
